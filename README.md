# attunenotes.github.io

Attune Notes helps you stay organized with simple, flexible lists. Create to-do lists, grocery lists, and reminders in seconds. You can also attach photos to your lists to make every item clearer and easier to remember.

Create Lists Instantly
Create to-do lists, grocery lists, reminders, or any checklist you need.

Add Photos
Attach photos to your lists for visual reminders and better organization.

Stay Organized
Keep tasks, shopping items, and notes all in one simple app.

Fast & Simple
No complicated setup. Just open the app and start creating lists.
